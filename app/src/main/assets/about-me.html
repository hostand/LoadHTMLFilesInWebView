About Sandip Bhattacharya

Sandip Bhattacharya is an independent Software Development Teacher, Developer, Online Course Creator, YouTuber from Kolkata, India. He has 14 years of overall programming and teaching experience in addition to Android apps and games development, Web development, and multiple programming languages like C, C++, Java, PHP/MySQL, etc. Sandip obtained his B. Tech and M. Tech degrees, both in Computer Science & Engineering, under West Bengal University of Technology, Kolkata.

He has been providing affordable and practical hands-on training, both in-person and online, on various software practices, and he has trained many aspirant students and software professionals, all over the world since 2006. He has also provided academic project guidance to computer science students (BE, B.Tech, BCA, MCA etc. and prepared freshers and experienced professionals for various technical job interviews and provided placement assistance for them. His past candidates include engineering students from many renowned universities in India as well as abroad and working professionals from major IT companies. Sandip now teaches on online learning markeplaces like Udemy and Skillshare, and on YouTube.
